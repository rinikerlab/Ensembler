-UnderConstuction- andersenThermostat This thermostat was developed by anderson 1980 and is also called stochastic collisions method. This method is optimal for single particle simulations as it tries to simulate a particle that collides with virtual particles from a temperature bath. At each collision, the velocity and the interval of the next collision is randomly reassigned and reselected from a Maxwell-Boltzmann distribution. This applying the thermostat leads to an at least microcanonical Simulation. Simulated energies should be gaussian distributed.
